Chase Atlantic

Chase Atlantic is an Australian band formed in 2014, known for blending contemporary R&B

with alternative pop and rock elements. The group consists of three core members:

Mitchel Cave, Clinton Cave, and Christian Anthony.

Meet The Members!

Mitchel Cave

Lead vocalist and programmer, born on December 2, 1996, making him 28 years old as of 2025. Before forming Chase Atlantic, Mitchel was part of the boy band What About Tonight, which competed in the 2012 season of The X Factor Australia.

Christian Anthony

Rhythm guitarist, lead vocalist, and programmer, born on September 3, 1997, making him 27 years old as of 2025. Christian also participated in The X Factor Australia in 2012 alongside Mitchel Cave.

Clinton Cave

Lead guitarist, tenor saxophonist, backing vocalist, and programmer, born on July 4, 1993, making him 31 years old as of 2025. Clinton initially gained recognition through his YouTube channel, where he posted music covers and original content.

About Chase Atlantic

Formed in Cairns, Australia, Chase Atlantic has developed a distinctive sound that blends dark alternative pop with elements of rock and R&B.

Over the years, they've evolved from their initial pop-rock beginnings to a more mature, alternative R&B style. Their influences include

artists like Tame Impala, The Weeknd, The 1975, and The Neighbourhood. The band has gained a dedicated fanbase through their dynamic music and engaging live performances.
